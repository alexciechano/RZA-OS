Our Animals


Explore a diverse collection of wildlife at our zoo! From majestic big cats like lions and tigers to the playful antics of primates, our animal residents span continents and ecosystems. Discover African elephants, graceful giraffes, and mighty rhinos in our savanna exhibit, or get up close to colorful birds and reptiles in our tropical zone. Our underwater exhibits feature seals, sea lions, and penguins, while our forest area is home to elusive wolves, bears, and deer. With over 200 species, there’s always something new to see and learn at our zoo!

Hotel Info


Stay close to the wild at our Zoo Lodge, located just steps away from all the exciting animal exhibits! Our family-friendly hotel offers comfortable rooms with beautiful views of the zoo grounds. Enjoy convenient amenities including a full-service restaurant, outdoor pool, and interactive play areas for kids. Guests also have access to exclusive after-hours tours, giving you a chance to see the animals in a unique, peaceful setting. Whether you’re visiting for a weekend getaway or a special occasion, the Zoo Lodge provides an unforgettable experience for all ages.
